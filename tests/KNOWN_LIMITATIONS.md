# AI Decision Intelligence Platform (ADIP)

# Known Limitations

Version: v1.0.0

---

The following limitations are acknowledged for the Research Edition.

## Functional Scope

- Designed primarily for research and architecture evaluation.
- Uses demonstration or representative data where applicable.
- Enterprise authentication is outside the scope of this version.
- Live ERP and CRM integrations are not included.
- Cloud-native production deployment is not implemented.
- Multi-tenant enterprise management is planned for future releases.

---

## Intellectual Property

- Patent documentation is frozen as a draft.
- Formal patent filing has not yet been completed.

---

## Future Development

Future enterprise editions may include:

- Enterprise authentication
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Production connectors
- Cloud deployment
- Industrial validation
- Commercial licensing
