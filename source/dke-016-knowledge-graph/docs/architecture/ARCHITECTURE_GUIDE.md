# Architecture Guide

## Overview

Project-1 is organized around modular decision intelligence capabilities. The platform separates knowledge construction, decision reasoning, governance, provenance, explainability, validation, documentation, patent preparation, research preparation, and commercial release packaging.

## Frozen Mechanisms

- DPG: tracks provenance relationships.
- DDGM: applies dynamic governance controls.
- TDLL: preserves temporal decision lineage.
- ADBM: models adaptive decision behavior.
- ADWG: represents adaptive workflow structure.
- DHMF: monitors decision health.
- DRIF: formats and routes decision recommendations.
- EDOF: orchestrates enterprise decision workflows.

## Backend Layers

- Research Layer
- Decision Knowledge Engine
- Decision Intelligence Engine
- Platform Integration
- Validation & Benchmarking
- Documentation
- Patent Preparation
- Research Paper Preparation
- Commercial Release

## Frontend Layer

UX-001 through UX-007 provide the enterprise user interface for dashboarding, decision workspaces, graph/provenance exploration, explainability, documentation generation, administration, and integrations.

## Scope Boundary

Enterprise data onboarding is intentionally outside Project-1.
