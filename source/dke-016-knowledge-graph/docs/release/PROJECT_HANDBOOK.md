# Project Handbook — Concise

## What Project-1 Is

Project-1 is an AI Decision Intelligence Platform. It helps represent, evaluate, explain, govern, and document decision workflows.

## What It Does

- Builds decision knowledge structures.
- Evaluates and ranks decision alternatives.
- Tracks provenance and lineage.
- Explains decisions and analytics.
- Supports documentation, patent, and research generation.
- Provides enterprise dashboard and administration UX.

## What It Does Not Do

It does not onboard real enterprise data, upload datasets, connect live external APIs, or ingest production data in Project-1.

## Why It Matters

The platform creates a traceable and explainable foundation for enterprise decision intelligence, suitable for future product, research, and patent development.
