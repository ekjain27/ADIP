# AI Decision Intelligence Platform (ADIP)

# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Version: v1.0.0

---

## What is ADIP?

ADIP (AI Decision Intelligence Platform) is a lifecycle-oriented enterprise decision intelligence platform designed to support transparent, explainable, governance-aware, and reproducible decision-making.

---

## Is ADIP a finished commercial product?

No.

Version 1.0.0 is the Research Edition.

It provides the reference architecture, implementation, and documentation that establish the foundation for future enterprise editions.

---

## Which industries can use ADIP?

The architecture is domain-independent.

Future integrations may support:

- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Finance
- Government
- Supply Chain
- Smart Cities
- Cybersecurity
- Retail
- Energy

---

## Does ADIP include real enterprise datasets?

No.

The Research Edition uses representative demonstration data for evaluation.

Future enterprise editions are expected to support organization-specific data through configurable connectors.

---

## Does ADIP currently support uploading datasets?

Not in Version 1.0.0.

Future versions are expected to provide support for importing data from CSV, Excel, databases, APIs, and enterprise platforms.

---

## Is authentication included?

No.

Authentication and Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) are planned for future enterprise releases.

---

## Is this release suitable for production?

No.

This release is intended for:

- Research
- Academic evaluation
- Architecture demonstration
- Technical validation
- Patent preparation
