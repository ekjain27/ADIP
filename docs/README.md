# Overview

The AI Decision Intelligence Platform (ADIP) is a lifecycle-oriented enterprise decision intelligence platform designed to support transparent, explainable, governance-aware, provenance-aware, and reproducible decision making.

Unlike traditional decision support systems that focus only on prediction or recommendation generation, ADIP coordinates the complete enterprise decision lifecycle through integrated knowledge engineering, contextual reasoning, governance enforcement, provenance tracking, workflow orchestration, validation, documentation, and deployment.
